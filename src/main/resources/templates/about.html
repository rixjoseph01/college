ABOUT OUR JOURNEY

POD7 COLLEGE OF MANAGEMENT AND APPLIED SCIENCES (Autonomous) was founded in 2021 and is affiliated to the ABCD University. It is owned by and managed by the POD7 GROUP through the Peermade Development Society. POD7 has a unique and enviable history. POD7 started with just three undergraduate programs and at present the college offers 20 programmes, including doctoral programs in Social Work and Commerce. POD7 was the youngest accredited* college in India by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) in 2021. The college won the prestigious status of the College with Potential for Excellence (CPE) from University Grants Commission (UGC) in 2020and 2021.
